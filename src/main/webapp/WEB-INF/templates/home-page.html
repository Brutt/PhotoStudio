About the photo shoot of newborns
and children up to a year

Age for shooting a newborn

The specifics of shooting newborns are available in a certain period - up to one month, and ideally from 5 to 14 days from birth. If you missed the deadlines, don’t worry, the main thing is to decide for yourself that you want to take professional photos of your child in infancy!

You can organize a photo shoot for any age. 1 month, 2,3,5, etc., because the charm of the baby every day only increases, and a child’s photographer will help you maintain these moments for many years!

Location of the photo shoot

A photo session can be held at home or in a specialized studio for newborns. We discuss all the nuances in a personal conversation.
